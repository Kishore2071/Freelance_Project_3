Healthcare FSA: 

You can use the Healthcare Flexible Spending Account to pay for uninsured health or dental plan expenses (such as co-payments or deductibles) for YOURSELF or YOUR DEPENDENTS.

Dependent Care FSA: 

Allows you to get reimbursed for qualifying expenses for your dependents, aged 12 or younger, for daycare, after school programs and summer camp or for eldercare.

Limited Purpose FSA

If you are enrolled in the CIGNA Core plan, you can enroll in this plan to pay for dental care, including co-payments and deductibles as well as for vision care.
